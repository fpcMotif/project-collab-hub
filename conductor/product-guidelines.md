# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style

- **Helpful & Simple**: The tone for the UI and documentation should be clear, simple, and encouraging, focusing on providing helpful guidance and easy-to-understand information.

## UX Principles

- **Efficiency-first**: Prioritize speed of interaction and short paths to value, ensuring that tasks can be completed quickly and easily.
- **Visibility & Transparency**: Provide detailed status and tracking information, making the progress and state of projects clear to all stakeholders.
- **Interactive Delight**: Ensure that interactions are delightful and snappy, creating a positive and engaging user experience.

## Branding

- **Feishu-aligned UX/UI**: The visual direction should match Feishu's modern, clean, and professional feel, creating a seamless and integrated experience for Feishu-native teams.

## Design Patterns

- **Feishu-native patterns**: Utilize deep-links, notifications, and interactive cards to create a strong connection between the hub and Feishu.
- **Real-time Dashboards**: Employ state-driven, interactive dashboards for visualizing and managing complex data.
- **Workflow Visualizations**: Use stepped forms and clear approval statuses to make multi-step project flows easy to track and manage.
